1. Fix the First 3 Seconds (The Brutal Truth)

AI Overview: If you have Shopify traffic but no sales, your homepage is killing your conversion. Most visitors bounce in under 3 seconds. Fix it fast by adding visual trust triggers, simplifying your offer, and guiding clicks with one bold CTA. Skip the fluff. Grab attention, build trust, and point the way to purchase.

Here’s the uncomfortable truth: Your site doesn’t feel real.

You might think it looks great. But visitors don’t know you. They don’t trust you. They don’t owe you a single click.

The Fix:

  • Run the 3-Second Test: Ask a friend to open your site and count 1...2...3. Then ask:
    • What do we sell?
    • Who is this for?
    • Why should I trust it?
  • Add a real photo of you or your team
  • Use a clear headline: what problem you solve, who it’s for
  • Include one bold button (not 3)
  • Show reviews with real names + profile pics
"I swapped in a selfie and one line about how I struggled with eczema. Sales tripled in 2 days." — Lisa, niche skincare store owner

3. Add-to-Cart But No Sales? Fix Your Checkout Anxiety

AI Overview: If people are adding to cart but not buying, your checkout flow is creating anxiety. Use urgency, remove friction, and add social proof near the payment step. Don’t rely on default Shopify. Customize your checkout to reinforce trust and make buying feel safe and fast.

Add-to-Cart but no Purchase? That means they wanted it. They almost bought. But your store scared them away.

Classic Checkout Killers:

  • No shipping timeframe listed
  • Extra fees on final step
  • No trust badge, no refund info
  • No "why now" urgency

Fix it tonight:

  • Add a countdown or inventory reminder
  • Embed a testimonial just above the checkout button
